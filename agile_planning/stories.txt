
Story 1:

As a user, I want to create a profile that I will use to log in.

Story 2: 

As a user, I want to keep a list of books that I want to read and be able to store who recommended that book.

Story 3:

As a user, I want to keep track of which books I have read so I can feel like I am making progress on reading the books I have.

Story 4:

As a user, I want to keep track of which books I like and jot down private notes/reflections about the book so I can look back and
recall my thoughts or feelings or what I learned while reading without the pressure of it being well written for others.

Story 5:

As a user, I want tp search books via genre or author and find books similar to books that I like.

Story 6:

As a user, I want to be able to use the service on my phone.
